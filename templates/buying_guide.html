Laptop Buying Guide (2025)

1. Processor (CPU)

Best choice: Intel i5 / Ryzen 5 for most users.

For gaming/editing: i7 / Ryzen 7.

For basic work: i3 / Ryzen 3.

2. RAM

• 8GB – Students, office work (minimum)

• 16GB – Programming, gaming, editing

• 32GB – Heavy creators, developers

3. Storage

Always prefer SSD over HDD.

512GB SSD recommended for most users.

4. GPU (Graphics Card)

For gaming: RTX 3050 / 4050 / 4060

For editing: GTX 1650 / RTX series

Normal usage: Integrated graphics is enough.

5. Display

• Full HD is minimum

• 144Hz or more for gaming

• IPS panels for best colors

6. Battery Life

Look for at least 6 hours backup

Ultrabooks ≈ 10–14 hours

7. Build Quality

Metal body > Plastic

Check hinge strength & keyboard comfort.

8. Future-Proof Features

✔ WiFi 6 / 6E

✔ USB-C charging

✔ Fingerprint sensor

✔ Backlit keyboard

Best Laptops Categories

Student Laptops

Affordable, good battery, portable.

Gaming Laptops

High GPU, cooling, high refresh display.

Programming Laptops

16GB RAM, SSD, good CPU.

Budget Laptops

Best value for basic work.
